About Dead Sould Game

The user must save from darkness a civilization of fishermen settled for millennia on a desert island hidden in the hollow of the Indian Ocean, where his boat ran aground. Our hero wakes up on the beach not remembering who he is. The player must then complete 4 levels, where during the day he completes quests in order to obtain clues to his true identity and that of his enemies and where at night he must fight monsters before they take full control of the world.

Gameplay

The more the levels advance, the more the monsters are resistant and in great number. The player must complete all his quests in order to access the final level where he will face the ruler of darkness. The player evolves in a constantly changing environment where the notion of day and night is very important.
